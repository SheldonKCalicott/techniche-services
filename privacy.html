Privacy Policy

How we protect your information

Information Collection

Personal Information: We only collect information you voluntarily provide through email contact or project inquiries. This may include your name, email address, and project details you choose to share.

Analytics Data: We use Vercel Analytics to understand website usage patterns. This collects anonymous data about page views, browser type, and general location (country/region) but no personally identifiable information.

No Tracking: We do not use cookies, third-party trackers, or collect personal data through automated means.

Information Use

Information you provide is used solely for:

  • Responding to your inquiries and project discussions
  • Providing requested services and deliverables
  • Maintaining professional relationships and project communication

No Sharing: Your personal information is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties without your explicit consent.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Security headers to prevent common web vulnerabilities
  • Content Security Policy (CSP) implementation
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Secure email handling and obfuscation techniques

Data Retention

Project-related communications are retained for the duration of our professional relationship and for reasonable business purposes thereafter. You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to information we have about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of any communications

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
